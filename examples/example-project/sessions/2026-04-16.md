# 2026-04-16 — v0.4.0 to staging

## Summary

Shipped magic-link auth end-to-end, benchmarked the sync engine against a 1000-note synthetic workspace, and promoted v0.4.0 to staging. Architect-review flagged the silent-conflict behavior as HIGH during ship review — filed Q6 (migration rollback plan) and decided to keep v0.4.0 on staging until the conflict-resolution UI ships.

## Key decisions

- **Promote to production only after conflict-resolution UI ships** (see `decisions.md` 2026-04-16). Silent conflict resolution is unacceptable for live users.

## Lessons / corrections

- Resend rate limiter caught us during the benchmark: CI was sending 50 magic-link emails per PR. Added a `RESEND_DRY_RUN=1` env guard for CI so the auth tests don't actually call Resend.
- `architect-review` in REVIEW mode caught the conflict behavior that `code-reviewer` missed. Reinforces the rule: always run architect-review, not just code-review, on structural changes.
- Monaco lag on 10k+ line notes surfaced during benchmarking — filed as Q3.
